Who We Are

Charsadda Coaching Academy was founded with one simple goal — to give every student in Charsadda access to the same quality of education available in big cities like Peshawar and Islamabad.

Since our founding, we have helped hundreds of students achieve top positions in Matric and FSc board exams, and gain admission into top un iversities and medical/engineering colleges across KPK.

We believe every student has potential. Our job is to unlock it.
